Welcome To
Kosgoda Turtle Hatchery

Kosgoda Turtle Hatchery, situated on the south coast of Sri Lanka near the city of Galle, is a significant nesting site for turtles . The hatchery plays a vital role in preserving the turtle population by providing a safe environment for nesting and hatching . It is known for its efforts to increase hatching rates and the care it provides to wounded, disabled, and weak turtles

What happens in the
Kosgoda Turtle Hatchery:

  • The hatchery is home to four out of the five turtle species found in Sri Lanka, including the rare "white turtle" Some of the species that nest on the pristine beaches of Kosgoda include Leatherback turtles
  • The volunteers and staff at Kosgoda Turtle Hatchery actively monitor the beaches at night, searching for mother turtles about to lay their eggs. Once the mother turtles are done laying eggs, the hatchery protects the eggs from potential threats and ensures a safe incubation period . They also care for injured turtles and provide necessary treatment.
  • Visitors to Kosgoda Turtle Hatchery have the opportunity to learn about turtle habitats, conservation efforts, and engage in activities related to turtle conservation. The hatchery welcomes both locals and tourists, offering short-term and long-term projects tailored to individual interests
