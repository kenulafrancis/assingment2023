Welcome to Hikkaduwa Turtle Hatchery

Welcome to the Hikkaduwa Turtle Hatchery, a renowned conservation project nestled along the captivating southwest coast of Sri Lanka. Our hatchery is dedicated to safeguarding and nurturing the precious sea turtle species that grace our shores. By visiting us, you embark on a captivating journey of exploration, education, and active participation in the conservation of these magnificent creatures.

Mission:At the Hikkaduwa Turtle Hatchery, our primary mission is to protect and preserve sea turtles, which are classified as endangered species. Through our dedicated efforts, we strive to create a safe haven for these remarkable creatures, ensuring their survival and contributing to the delicate balance of marine ecosystems.

Turtle Hatchery Tour:

Educational Programs:
