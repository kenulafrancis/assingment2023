We would appreciate if you can make a donation and contribute towards saving turtle.
Know what happens to your donations:

  • Responsible turtle hatcheries aim to use the funds generated from visitors to support conservation initiatives. These hatcheries strive to protect sea turtles and their nesting habitats, raise awareness about turtle conservation, conduct research, and implement measures to ensure the survival and growth of turtle populations
  • The funds are used to maintain the hatchery facilities, provide necessary care for injured or weak turtles, protect nests from threats, support monitoring and research programs, educate the public about turtle conservation, and contribute to local community development initiatives
